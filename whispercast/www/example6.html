Here we have an example of Picture-In-Picture:

  • A global FileElement (named 'examplek') reads the files. We have two different playlists played by a global SwitchingElement (similar with example4).
  • One cannot search in individual files, and the bar indicates the progress in the current clip of the playlist.
  • Everything is RTMP exported to the user via a NormalizingElement.
You need Flash Player.
